Social Media Do's and Don'ts for Students

DO's:

Do use privacy settings to restrict who can see your personal posts.

Do think before you post; remember that the internet is permanent.

Do use platforms like LinkedIn to share academic projects and mechanical engineering interests.

Do verify the credibility of news or articles before sharing them.

Do treat others with respect and engage constructively in comment sections.

DON'Ts:

Don't post pictures of your college ID card, tickets, or documents showing personal information.

Don't engage in heated arguments or cyberbullying.

Don't complain about professors, peers, or college administration publicly.

Don't accept friend requests from completely unknown profiles.

Don't post unprofessional content that you wouldn't want a future internship coordinator to see.
