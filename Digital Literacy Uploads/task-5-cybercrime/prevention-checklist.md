Stay Safe Online: Student Prevention Checklist

Use strong, unique passwords for every account and enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).

Never enter your UPI PIN to receive money; PINs are only required to send money.

Do not click on unverified links sent via SMS or WhatsApp promising cashbacks or rewards.

Keep your operating systems, browsers, and antivirus software updated.

Avoid doing banking transactions over public, unsecured college Wi-Fi networks.

Regularly back up your important academic documents and project files to a cloud service to prevent ransomware loss.

Be cautious of phishing emails that mimic university administrative alerts requiring urgent login.

Only download software, games, and apps from official, verified sources.
